Foie Gras Au Torchon

$48

a luxurious and refined French delicacy. It involves a preparation method where the foie gras (goose or duck liver) is gently seasoned, rolled into a cylindrical shape, and tightly wrapped in a cloth or plastic wrap. The liver is then poached in a rich and flavorful liquid, resulting in a velvety and buttery texture. Served chilled, Foie Gras Au Torchon offers a delicate and complex flavor profile, often accompanied by sweet or tangy accompaniments like fruit compotes or balsamic reductions. This dish is a true indulgence for those seeking an elegant culinary experience.
