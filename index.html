About Eat the Rude

   Indulge in a twisted culinary journey at Eat the Rude, a avant-garde restaurant helmed by the enigmatic Hannibal Lecter. Delight in a symphony of flavors, starting with the infamous "Fava Bean and Chianti Pâté" and its ironic undertones. The "Sweetbreads Surprise" offers a harmonious blend of creamy sweetbreads and tangy raspberry reduction, while the daring "Liver Symphony" combines foie gras and sautéed chicken livers.

   Let Hannibal guide you through an extensive wine selection, perfectly paired with each course for an elevated experience. Desserts like the "Red Velvet Surprise" with its blood-red center and the tantalizing "Silence of the Lambs" showcase Hannibal's artistic touch. Before you leave, explore the in-house gift shop to take home exclusive culinary items. Eat the Rude promises a dining experience that will linger in your memory.
